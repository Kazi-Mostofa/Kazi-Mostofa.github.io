About Me

Graduate Research Assistant at Clarkson University pursuing a PhD in Electrical & Computer Engineering, specializing in renewable energy systems, microgrid optimization, and both deterministic and probabilistic load forecasting.

My research focuses on developing intelligent energy systems that integrate machine learning for energy modeling, smart building analytics, and IoT-based monitoring. I have published extensively in energy data science, optimization algorithms, and sustainable infrastructure, contributing to the advancement of clean energy technologies and grid modernization.
