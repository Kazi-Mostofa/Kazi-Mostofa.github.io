About Me

Graduate Research Assistant at Clarkson University, specializing in sustainable energy systems and intelligent grid optimization.

I am pursuing a PhD in Electrical & Computer Engineering with a focus on developing cutting-edge solutions for renewable energy integration, microgrid economic dispatch, and advanced load forecasting. My research combines machine learning techniques with energy systems analysis to create intelligent, efficient, and sustainable energy infrastructure.

With extensive experience in energy data science, optimization algorithms, and IoT-based monitoring systems, I contribute to the advancement of clean energy technologies and smart grid modernization. My work has been published in leading journals and has direct applications in real-world energy systems, from building-level analytics to utility-scale microgrids.
